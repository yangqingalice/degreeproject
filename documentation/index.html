Week1

Z Axis

Every physical object has three dimensions, width on the x axis, height on the y axis and thickness on the z axis. A single sheet of flat paper has a z axis that is almost unnoticeable, but when more of them are sequenced together, the z axis makes all the difference. What is the z axis within various forms and media and how it functions?

What makes a book a book, instead of a pile of pages?

Ulises Carrion said in The New Art of Making Books,

“A book is a sequence of spaces. Each of these spaces is perceived at a different moment - a book is also a sequence of moments. A book is not a case of words, nor a bag of words, nor a bearer of words.

Written language is a sequence of signs expanding within the space; the reading of which occurs in the time. A book is a space-time sequence.”

What happens if the value of z axis is zero?

In mathematics, the natural number are those used for counting and ordering. Some definitions begin the natural number with 1, not 0. Is 0 in its basis not natural? Does it exists in the natural world of art making? What is a book with 0 page?

What happens if the value of z axis is infinite?

The physicality of book as a medium almost always gives it a fixed amount of pages. However when we look into the fictional world inside the screens, infinity is everywhere.

This project starts with the exploration of form, with no specific social purpose. It is, for now, an exploration of pure self-interest. After re-visiting some of my personal favorite projects made in the past two and a half years, I found the study of form, material and media to be the more pleasant field. The result of the project may be socially meaningless and non-functional, which I would enjoy. However there is still possibility if any specific topic comes up that will go with the exploration of z axis.

Some extra thoughts on z axis if it’s defined as a system.

System is a series of arbitrary decisions.
System frees people from making more arbitrary decisions.
System is repetition.
System is possibility. It generates different results.

Interactive Instinct on Web

More thoughts on the updated stone project

Without arbitrary rectangles as keys of the stone instrument, the experience becomes even closer to the real physical experience; however at the same time, the clarity of visual instruction is sacrificed. The initial interaction becomes less instant. User’s natural instinct comes into play and encourages him or her to press on a plain image without any buttons.
